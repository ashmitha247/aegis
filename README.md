# Aegis 🛡️

**Aegis** is a nonprofit, community-driven women’s health platform designed to reduce stigma, misinformation, delayed diagnosis, and dismissive healthcare experiences in gynecology. 

Unlike traditional femtech apps focused solely on fertility, Aegis prioritizes emotional safety, healthcare self-advocacy, and collective feminine wisdom.

---

## 🌟 Overview
Inspired by ancient healing sanctuaries, Aegis combines modern technology with community-centered healthcare navigation. We focus on:
* **Emotional Safety:** Providing a secure space for sensitive health discussions.
* **Self-Advocacy:** Tools to help women feel heard and prepared for medical consultations.
* **Trusted Guidance:** Crowdsourced recommendations based on empathy and patient experience.
* **Symptom Mastery:** Beyond tracking—providing intelligent insights and analytics.

---

## ⚠️ The Problem
Women often normalize severe pain or hesitate to discuss gynecological issues due to societal stigma. This leads to:
> "Many women experience delayed diagnoses and feel unheard during consultations, relying on fragmented online information."

Aegis acknowledges that women already depend on friends and support groups to navigate healthcare safely; we simply organize that trusted support into one accessible platform.

---

## 🛠️ Core Features

### 📊 Symptom Journal & Health Analytics
Track pain, fatigue, bleeding, mood, and hormonal symptoms.
* **Visual Insights:** Circular cycle visualizations and timeline-based graphs.
* **AI Summaries:** Automatically generated appointment summaries to identify patterns.
* **Goal:** Bridge the communication gap between patients and doctors.

### 🏛️ Shared Wisdom (Directory)
A community-curated space for gynecologist recommendations. This is **not** a toxic rating platform; it is a space to find:
* **Empathetic Care:** Filters for "good listener," "PCOS-aware," or "comfortable for first visits."
* **Context:** Supportive community notes and specialty-based tags.

### 🗣️ Anonymous Community Support
Safe, topic-based spaces to ask questions, share lived experiences, and seek emotional support without judgment.

### 📚 Educational Hub
Evidence-based, culturally sensitive education on menstrual health, endometriosis, myths, and healthcare awareness.

### 📝 Appointment Preparation Tool
Transforms your raw symptom history into structured, doctor-ready notes and suggested questions to maximize your consultation time.
